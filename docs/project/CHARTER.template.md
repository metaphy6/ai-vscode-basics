# 📜 Charter: `<project name>`

> Copy to `docs/project/CHARTER.md`. One screen, max.

## Vision

One sentence: the long-term state of the world this project is trying to bring about.

## Scope (in)

- What this project will deliver, bulleted.
- The 3-5 user-visible outcomes that define "done enough to ship".

## Scope (out)

- What this project will NOT do, with the reason next to it.
- Calls out scope-creep traps and adjacent problems we're aware of but won't solve.

## Audience

- **Primary**: who uses this?
- **Secondary**: who else touches it?
- **Operators**: who runs it in production?

## Success criteria

How will we know this worked? Concrete, measurable.

## Non-goals & explicit trade-offs

What are we accepting as a cost? (e.g. "Performance is secondary to clarity for the first 6 months.")

## Constraints

Hard constraints we cannot break (legal, technical, organisational).
